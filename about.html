Our Beginning

Born From a Passion
for Global Flavour

◆

Banjara is a premium dining destination offering a curated global buffet experience that celebrates the rich tapestry of world cuisines. Our name — meaning "nomad" in Hindi — reflects our spirit of culinary exploration across borders and traditions.

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Ahmedabad, we set out to create a space where families, couples, and connoisseurs could gather around an extraordinary table — one that speaks every culinary language with equal fluency and love.

What Defines Us

Our Three Pillars

◆

Everything we do is guided by three core commitments that define the Banjara standard.

Luxury Ambience

◆

Our interiors were designed by award-winning architects to envelope guests in warmth, sophistication, and understated grandeur. From the soft lighting to the handpicked art, every detail invites you to linger longer.

Expert Chefs

◆

Our brigade of 25+ culinary artists hail from Michelin-starred kitchens across India and the world. Each brings a unique perspective that enriches our buffet with authentic technique and creative passion.

Diverse Cuisine

◆

From the richness of Mughlai curries to the delicacy of Japanese sushi, Italian risottos to Levantine mezze — our buffet is a genuine world tour on a single plate, refreshed seasonally.

Our Mission

Crafting Moments
that Last Forever

◆

At Banjara, we believe a meal is never just food — it is memory, emotion, and connection. Whether you are celebrating a milestone, hosting a corporate gathering, or simply treating yourself, we ensure that every moment at our table is extraordinary.

Our promise is simple: exceptional quality, impeccable service, and an atmosphere that makes every guest feel like royalty. We do not just serve food — we craft experiences that you will want to relive again and again.
